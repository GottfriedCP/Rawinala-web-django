SERVICE

You can be involved in Rawinala's services through the following programs:

  • Foster Parents Program (Schools and Dormitories). Most of Rawinala's children come from families with weak economies. They need your helping hand to continue to school and get quality care.
  • Education and Care Worker Welfare. Educators and caregivers are the main force in all Rawinala's activities. You can help to improve their welfare through this program.
  • Teacher Training at the Training Center. Through this program, you can help children with multiple disabilities in the supported regions to be able to access education.

DONATION
