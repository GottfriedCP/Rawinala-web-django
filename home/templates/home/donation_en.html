SERVICE

You can be involved in Rawinala's services through the following programs:

  • Foster Parents Program (Schools and Dormitories). Most of Rawinala's children come from poor families. They need your hand for a help to get a good education and get a better care.
  • Educators and Care Workers Welfare. Educators and care workers are the main role in all Rawinala's activities. You can participate in improving their welfare through this program.
  • Teacher Training at the Training Center. Through this program, you can help the children with multiple disabilities in the regions to be able to get a better education.
